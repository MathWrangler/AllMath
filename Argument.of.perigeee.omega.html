This equation computes the argument of perigee (argument of periapsis), `omega`, of an orbit. == INPUTS[[image:Orbital Parameters.png||style="float: right;"]] == The argument of perigee is computed from the inputs: * `n_x` - the x-component of the nodal vector * `n` - the nodal vector's magnitude == NOTES == The Argument of Perigee is the angle measured from the Ascending Node to the periapsis, (perigee point), along the satellite's direction of travel.
